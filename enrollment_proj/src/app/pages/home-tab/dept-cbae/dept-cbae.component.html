PROGRAMS OFFERED

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administraion Major in Marketing Management
    (BSBA-MM)

    A program that prepares students for a marketing career in various organizations and businesses.

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administraion Major in Financial Management
    (BSBA-FM)

    A degree program that offers students a comprehensive understanding of financial decision-making. It covers theories, principles, and concepts essential for navigating both local and international business landscapes

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administraion Major in Human Resource Development Management
    (BSBA-HRDM)

    An undergraduate program that focuses on the strategic management of human resources within organizations. It covers topics such as recruitment, training, performance management, employee relations, and organizational development.
