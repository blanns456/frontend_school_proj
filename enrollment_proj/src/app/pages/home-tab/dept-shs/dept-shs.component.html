STRANDS OFFERED

  • Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics (STEM)

    The STEM strand in senior high school introduces students to advanced mathematical and scientific theories and concepts, laying the groundwork for their future college studies.

  • Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMMS)

    This academic strand embraces a diverse array of disciplines aimed at exploring the complexities of human conditions. It engages students in the exploration and investigation of human behavior and societal changes using empirical, analytical, and critical methodologies.

  • General Academics Strand (GAS)

    A strand that takes a general approach to prepare students for university life. Students can expect to tackle different disciplines. This can cover various subjects and topics such as Humanities, Management, Social Sciences, and even Organization.

  • Accountancy Business and Management(ABM)

    The strand paves a way for students in taking up a Business Administration degree. It focuses on teaching them the basics of financial management, project management, marketing, human resources, modern management trends, organizational behavior, and more.

  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding(SMAW)

    A technical Diploma program provides a solid foundation in process-specific production welding technology.

  • Information Communication Technology(ICT)

    A strand that equips you with advanced skills in computer systems, programming, creating web pages, and basic animation.
