Andres Soriano College has a variety of CHED recognized undergraduate programs in its three departments: Arts & Education, Business & Management, and Computer Science. The school also has a Senior High School department offering ABM, HUMSS, GAS, HE, and IA. Technical courses from TESDA, like Commercial Cooking, Computer Programming, and Food & Beverage Services, are also available for those who would like to get their National Certificate (NC).
